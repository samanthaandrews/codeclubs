CodeClubs.org is a nonprofit working to strengthen the STEM workforce in New Mexico through free education and training.

We are currently partnered with Girls Who Code, a national organization working to close the gender gap in technology and change the image of what a programmer looks like and does.
Check back soon for free coding clubs for everyone!
Facilitate a club

Facilitators must be women who have a basic understanding of computer programming concepts.

Must be able to spend at least 2 hours per week in the classroom for a full semester (16-weeks).

We have a curriculum library that you are welcome to use, or you can make your own!

We will work with you to make sure you have the necessary supplies (computers, snacks, etc.) but facilitators are responsible for securing a location.

Volunteer to be a speaker

The first 30 minutes of each club is dedicated to a Women in Tech Spotlight, where women with careers in tech give a short presentation to the club.

If you'd like to be added to our list of speakers, fill out the form and we will get in touch with you.
